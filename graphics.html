Evaluation of web graphics

Webpage: Homepage - Hawkeye Community College

The graphics on this webpage seem to be mainly used for conveying information.

All of the graphics contrast well with the website's background, and most of them have alternate text to describe them. The "commencement ceremony" image under the news section, however, doesn't have alternate text. The graphic doesn't really seem to help the page, considering a heading that mentions the commencement ceremony is right below it.
